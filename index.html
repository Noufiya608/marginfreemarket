About Marginfree Market

Three decades of excellence in retail

Our Story

Founded in 1994 and incorporated on October 24, 2000, Marginfree Market Pvt. Ltd. revolutionized Kerala's retail landscape by introducing the margin-free concept. We were the first supermarket chain in Kerala to offer products directly from manufacturers at margin-free rates.

With over three decades of service, we have grown from a single store to 350+ franchise locations across Kerala, maintaining our commitment to providing high-quality products at the lowest possible prices.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality food and essentials at the lowest prices through direct sourcing, empowering consumers with maximum value.

Our Vision

To empower consumers through ethical retail practices, making quality products accessible to every household in Kerala.
